Chapter 1: The Malice Inconnu

An Anthology Tale

The iron was stubborn tonight, resisting the heat of the coals as if it knew the grim work it was destined for.

"It won't take the shape," the apprentice whispered, leaning away from the intense glare of the forge.

The master smith didn't look up, his hammer poised. "It will take it. It must."

***

Hours later, the blade rested in the oil, a dark reflection rippling along its fuller...
